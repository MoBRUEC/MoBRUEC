How to Connect

1
Open this page in two separate browser tabs or devices
2
In the first tab, click "Generate Peer ID" and copy the generated ID
3
In the second tab, paste the copied ID into the "Remote Device" area and click "Connect to Peer"
4
Wait for the connection to establish (you'll see "Connected to peer" on both tabs)
5
Once connected, you can securely transfer files between devices!
